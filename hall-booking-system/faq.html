Quick answers about booking, payments, cancellations, and event planning.

Booking & Availability

You can book the hall through our website by selecting your preferred date, filling in the booking form, and submitting your request. Once confirmed, all your booking details will be saved in your booking history for easy reference.

You can check the availability of the hall by visiting our website and selecting your preferred date. If the hall is available, you will be able to proceed with the booking process.

Payments & Pricing

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and e-wallet.

Cancellation & Changes

Cancellations must be made at least 3 days before the booking date. Any cancellation made after the deadline may be subject to a cancellation fee or partial charge.
