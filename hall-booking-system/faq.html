Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about booking, payments, cancellations, and event planning.

Booking & Availability

Pick a venue on the homepage, then choose your event date and payment method on the booking page. After you reserve, your booking details will appear in Your Bookings.

Availability is confirmed during the reservation step. If the selected hall can’t accept bookings for your date, you’ll be asked to choose another date.

Yes. Most venues support multiple event types such as weddings, corporate meetings, exhibitions, and celebrations. Check the venue details and facilities on the booking page.

Payments & Pricing

You can pay using Online Banking (FPX), Credit/Debit Card, or E-Wallet (TNG/Grab). (Your choice is shown in your booking history.)

Yes. The hall price shown on the homepage and booking sidebar is the cost for 1 day, and it includes taxes and charges.

For this demo flow, no prepayment is needed. Your reservation is saved and displayed under Your Bookings. (You can adjust this behavior to match your real payment system later.)

Cancellation & Changes

You can cancel up to 7 days before the event date for a free cancellation. Cancel options are available in Your Bookings.

To change the date, cancel your current booking (if allowed), then create a new booking with your preferred date. In the full version, you can implement date-change functionality.

Your booking will be removed from the history list. If this is your latest reservation, the upcoming booking reminder will also clear.

Venue Details

Open a hall listing, then check the Property Highlights, Most Popular Facilities, and House Rules sections on the booking page.

Decoration is optional. If you enable the decoration package on the booking sidebar, it adds +RM 200 and lets you choose a theme.

House rules can vary per venue. Always review Check-in, Check-out, Smoking, and Cancellation sections in the hall details.
